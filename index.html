Sets overall HR direction for PGB Group. You align MHG's people strategy to this center.

PGB Culture & How We Work
Culture is not written in a policy manual. It lives in how people behave when no one is watching, how decisions are made under pressure, and what gets celebrated and what gets tolerated.
The 3 PGB Core Values — In Depth

PAGSALIG

Trust
  • Trust is earned through consistency, honesty, and follow-through.
  • When you say something will be done, do it. When you can't, say so immediately.
  • Nurses and doctors trust HR when HR understands their world — not just HR policy.
  • Watch how leaders speak about employees who raise concerns. That is Pagsalig in action.

HINIUSANG PAGKUGI

Collective Effort
  • No one person carries the organization. We win as a team.
  • Your programs will only succeed if department heads co-own them with you.
  • Teamwork in a hospital is not optional — mirror that in your HR approach.
  • Notice how credit is shared in meetings. PGB values team recognition.

PAGPALAMBO

Growth
  • Committed to continuous improvement — of people, systems, and the organization.
  • You will be expected to grow your own skills as you grow others.
  • CPD is not just compliance — it signals that MHG invests in clinical professionals.
  • Leaders who develop their teams are celebrated. Managers who hoard talent are challenged.
6 Things That Define How PGB Works
1

We are Conglomerate Builders

You work across Construction, Real Estate, Maritime, Healthcare, and Education. Embrace the breadth — it is a strength, not a complexity.

2

We Lead with Values, Not Rules

Pagsalig, Hiniusang Pagkugi, and Pagpalambo are the operating system of how we work. When in doubt, return to the values.

3

We Partner Across Boundaries

No SBU operates in isolation. HR is a shared service and a strategic function simultaneously. Your best work will come from cross-SBU collaboration.

4

We Grow People Deliberately

Pagpalambo is not a benefit — it is an expectation. We invest in people because organizational growth is inseparable from personal growth.

5

We Take Ownership Seriously

The EOS framework drives how we run our teams: clear Rocks, L10 meetings, and accountability culture. Own your commitments.

6

We Care for the Whole Person

Wellbeing, dignity, and psychological safety are not HR programs — they are cultural non-negotiables at MHG.

Culture Do's & Don'ts at MHG
✓ DO
Speak up in meetings — your HRSP perspective is valued
Build relationships before you build programs
Bring data to every leadership conversation
Respect the clinical hierarchy — MHG has a strong medical culture
Celebrate small wins publicly and correct privately
Ask 'How can HR help?' before saying 'Here's what HR requires'
✗ DON'T
Assume what worked at your last company will work here
Bypass department heads — always keep them in the loop
Make promises HR can't keep
Treat clinical staff and admin staff the same — their realities differ
Underestimate the power of informal influence in healthcare

The HR Strategic Partner Mindset
These are the non-negotiable principles that should guide how you operate across all 100 days — especially in a healthcare organization where people directly affect patient care quality.
🩺

Healthcare First

Every HR decision has a downstream effect on patient care quality. Staffing gaps, burnout, and disengagement are not just HR problems — they are patient safety issues. Think clinical impact in every people decision.

👂

Listen Before Acting

The biggest mistake a new HRSP makes is launching initiatives before understanding context. Spend your first 25 days listening more than talking. Earn the right to change things.

📊

Evidence-Based Decisions

Never advocate for a program or policy without data to support it. Build the habit of showing metrics, citing sources, and tracking outcomes. HR credibility is built through numbers, not instincts alone.

🤝

Partner, Not Policeman

Department heads should see you as someone who helps them solve people problems — not a compliance enforcer. Build trust by understanding their business realities before imposing HR realities.

🌱

PGB Core Values as North Star

Pagsalig, Hiniusang Pagkugi, and Pagpalambo are not slogans. Embed them into every HR program — from recruitment criteria to performance ratings to recognition standards.

⚡

Deliver Early, Deliver Visibly

Quick wins matter. Within the first 60 days, launch at least one visible, positive HR improvement that employees can feel and talk about. This builds momentum and political capital.

🔒

Confidentiality as Sacred Trust

In a healthcare setting, staff share sensitive personal and professional information. Handle every piece of employee data with the same confidentiality you would apply to patient records.
